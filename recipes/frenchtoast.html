French Toast

Description

French toast is a dish made by dipping slices of bread in a mixture of beaten eggs, and often milk or cream, then frying them in butter or oil.
It's a popular breakfast or brunch item, typically served with toppings like syrup, fruit, or powdered sugar.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon powder
  • Salt
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Slowly whisk the milk into the flour.
  3. Then whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  4. Heat the pan with little oil or butter. Meanwhile, soak the bread slices in the milk until saturated.
  5. Cook the slices on the preheated pan until golden brown on each side.
  6. Serve hot & enjoy.
