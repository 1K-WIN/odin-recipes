Apple Pie

Description

Apple pie is a baked dessert consisting of a pastry crust filled with cooked apples, typically seasoned with sugar, cinnamon, and other spices.
It's a popular dessert, especially in English-speaking countries, and is often served warm or cold, sometimes with whipped cream, ice cream, or cheese.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Unsalted butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Double-crust pie pastry, thawed

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients and preheat the oven.
  2. Make a paste by mixing flour, melted butter, sugar & water over a heating pan.
  3. Press one crust into a pie plate. Lay all the sliced apples on the bottom crust.
  4. Assemble the top crust by making the lattice crust.
  5. Pour sugar-butter mixture over lattice crust, making sure it seeps over sliced apples.
  6. Bake the pie in a preheated oven until the apples are soft and the crust is golden brown.
